Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 2026

This privacy policy describes how the 4D Prize Calculator (4D万字奖金计算器) app handles your information.

No Data Collection

This app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. We do not gather any information about you or your device.

Local Processing

All calculations are performed entirely on your device. No data is sent to any server.

Local Storage

Calculation history is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device and can be cleared at any time.

No Third-Party Services

  • No analytics or tracking tools
  • No advertising networks
  • No third-party SDKs that collect data

No Internet Required

This app works entirely offline. No internet connection is required to use any feature. A brief internet connection may be used on first launch to load a splash video, but no personal data is transmitted.

Contact
